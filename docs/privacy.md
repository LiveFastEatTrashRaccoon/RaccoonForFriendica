# Privacy Policy

*Last Updated: May, 12 2026*

## 1. General information

This website is a static site designed to provide information without tracking its visitors. We take
your privacy seriously and do not collect, store, or share any personal information.

## 2. Data collection and usage

- **Cookies:** this website does not use cookies.
- **Tracking:** we do not use any analytics, tracking pixels, or advertising scripts.
- **Server logs:** our hosting provider (GitHub Pages) may process standard server logs, which
  include your IP address, to ensure the security and performance of the site.
  This data is not used to identify individuals.

## 3. Third-party services

This site does not embed third-party content (like YouTube videos or Google Maps) that could set
third-party cookies or track your behavior.

## 4. Your rights (GDPR)

Under the GDPR, you have the right to be informed that no data is being collected. Since we do not
store personal data, we cannot provide access to, rectify, or delete any user-specific
information.
